Investing.com:
Investing.com also provides historical exchange rate data for the euro and Russian ruble in CSV format.
To download the data, go to the historical data page for the EUR/RUB currency pair at
https://www.investing.com/currencies/eur-rub-historical-data
and select "Download Data" at the bottom of the page.
Then, select "CSV" as the download format and choose the desired time period.
